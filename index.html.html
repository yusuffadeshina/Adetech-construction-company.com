Our Services

Iron Gates

All our gates are carefully and well fabricated using high quality materials that withstand the test of time:the thickness of the frames ranges from 2mm-3mm,the iron sheets guage ranges from 2.5mm-3mm depending on the demands, this also apply to laser cuts plate,designer plate or plain plate. Moreover,we carefully mount our gate tracks,making sure that little force is applied either when opening or closing using remote or manually.All these features serve the purpose of securing the premises of the building and also adding beauty and vibe to it because the colour is carefully selected to blends with the colour of the building.

Car Ports

Our car port compete with our foreign counterparts,on completion of project it looks like imported ones. Our materials are carefully selected and we implore high professionals to achieve the best results; depending on your choice we have one carport,two carport,three carport,four carport.This varies due to available space of car or choice.

Water Tank Stands

Water tanks and tank stand often,although not always go hand in hand.The decision whether or not to install your water tank on a tank stand depends on a number of factors; It needs to be placed on a firm level surface. Tank stands are available in a range of heights: if you want to make use of gravity for water pressure,the higher the stand,the more the pressure will be generated.
